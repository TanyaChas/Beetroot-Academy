ROY BATTY, 2019

LIKE TEARS IN RAIN

Where am I going? How long have I go?

I`ve seen things you people wouldn`t believe. Attack ships on fire odd the shoulder of Orion. I watched C-beams glitter in the dark near the Tannhauser gate. All those moments will be lost in time ... like tears in rain... Time to die.

I don`t know why he saved my life. Maybe in those moments he loved life more than he ever had before. Not just his life - anybody`s life; my life. All he`d wanted were the same answers...
